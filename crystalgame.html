Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below.
By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.
You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it.
Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will chnge.
